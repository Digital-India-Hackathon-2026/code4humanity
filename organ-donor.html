Common concerns

Myth vs. fact

Organ donation is one of the most misunderstood medical decisions — here's the reality.

Myth

Doctors won't try as hard to save my life if I'm a registered donor.

Fact

Medical teams treating you are always separate from transplant teams. Saving your life comes first, always.

Myth

I'm too old or unhealthy to donate.

Fact

There's no strict age cutoff — medical suitability is assessed at the time of donation, not at registration.

Myth

My family can't be involved in the decision.

Fact

We strongly encourage you to tell your family your wishes — it makes the process easier for everyone.
