The process

From search to saved life, in five steps

Each step below happens in order — the sequence itself is what makes a match possible within minutes, not days.

STEP 1
🔍

Search

Enter blood type or organ need — LifeLink scans verified donors nearby.

STEP 2
🧬

Match

Compatibility, distance, and urgency are weighed to rank the best matches.

STEP 3
🔔

Notify

Matched donors and the requesting hospital are alerted instantly.

STEP 4
💉

Donate

Donation is scheduled at the nearest verified hospital or blood bank.

STEP 5
❤️

Save Life

The recipient is treated — and the outcome is logged back to the network.
