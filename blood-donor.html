Before you register

Am I eligible to donate?

Most healthy adults qualify. Here's the quick checklist LifeLink and partner hospitals use.

🎂

Age 18–65

First-time donors should be within this range; repeat donors may extend slightly with medical clearance.

⚖️

50+ kg body weight

A minimum weight ensures donation is safe for both donor and recipient.

❤️

Good general health

No fever, infection, or major surgery in the last 6 months.

🗓️

12+ weeks since last donation

Whole blood donors need at least 3 months between donations to recover safely.
