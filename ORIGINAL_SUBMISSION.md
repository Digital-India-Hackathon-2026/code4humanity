## Idea
LifeLink is an AI-powered blood and organ donor network connecting donors, hospitals, blood banks, and patients in real time. It uses GPS-based smart matching, verified requests, emergency SOS alerts, digital donor IDs, appointment scheduling, eligibility reminders, and AI-powered blood shortage prediction to ensure faster emergency response and save more lives.
